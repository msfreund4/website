## Welcome to the Freund Group

We are a research group in the [Department of Chemistry](https://www.dal.ca/faculty/science/chemistry.html) at **Dalhousie University** in Halifax, Nova Scotia, led by **Professor Michael S. Freund**.

Our research spans three interconnected themes:

- **Sustainable Fuels** — Membrane and electrode technologies for green hydrogen production and solar water splitting.
- **Machine Olfaction** — Electronic nose systems using conducting polymer sensor arrays combined with AI for vapor recognition.
- **Polymer Electronics** — Synthesis, characterization, and application of conducting and self-doped polymers.
